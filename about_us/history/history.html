History

Since 1987, we have been trying to provide our customers the perfect cup of coffee and quality food that satisfy your taste buds. The owner of this cafe is Ang Wei Jin. After years of research and hardwork, our team are able to start this cafe and offer quality goods for our customers.


We are working together to bring you the best of coffee and food. Our team handpicked all the coffee beans used to ensure quality and taste. We have been roasting the coffee beans ourselves to bring out the full flavor and give our customers a great experience while tasting our products.


This website serves to allow customers to check our products online so they can know more about our products and services. We offer a wide range of drinks and foods for our customers.
